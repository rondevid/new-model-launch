Genre Transformation Engine

How it Works

Examples

  • CHIPTUNE FRENZY: Create an upbeat, surprisingly funny Chiptune version of the "Imperial March" using SID-like sounds.

Focus: B2C/Prosumer (Remixers, Streamers); B2B (Themed campaigns, Unique Sound Design).

TRANSLAIT - Music Beyond Language Barriers

Translate lyrics and vocal delivery style across languages, maintaining musicality, rhythm, and emotional intent for global accessibility.

How it Works

AI analyzes source lyrics (meaning, rhyme, rhythm), melody & vocal delivery > Translates lyrics accurately > Adapts melody and phrasing subtly to fit the cadence, phonetics, and natural rhythm of the target language.

  • Maintains emotional core and intent
  • Adjusts note timing and duration for linguistic flow
  • Can optionally leverage AI voice synthesis tuned to original style/gender
  • Considers rhyme scheme and syllable count where appropriate

Examples

  • "Fly Me to the Moon" (English): Convincingly rendered in Hindi with appropriate romantic phrasing, or in Mandarin Chinese capturing the aspiration smoothly.
  • "Knockin' on Heaven's Door" (English): Translate to Japanese maintaining the melancholic, seeking tone, or to German preserving the simple, profound feel within the language's structure.

Focus: Strong B2B (Publishers, Labels, Film/Ad Dubbing), B2C (Cross-cultural sharing), Trend for global music reach.

RE-MOOD & RE-GENRE - Adaptive Music DNA

Intelligently adapt the core musical identity (hook melody, harmony, rhythm) of a track to entirely new moods or genres, preserving its recognizable essence.

How it Works

AI identifies key musical elements ("Music DNA") > User selects new target mood (e.g., "uplifting," "suspenseful") and/or genre (e.g., "Orchestral," "Synthwave") > AI regenerates track, transforming tempo, instrumentation, harmony, rhythm, and effects accordingly.

  • Re-interprets emotional core based on mood selection
  • Adjusts structural conventions to fit genre norms
  • Retains core melodic hooks for recognizability
  • Modifies harmony (e.g., minor vs major keys for mood)

Examples

  • Upbeat Corporate Melody > Re-Mood to "Suspenseful": Slow down tempo, shift to minor key variations, add tense strings, pulsing low bass, and reverb.
  • Catchy Pop Hook > Re-Genre to "Epic Orchestral": Arrange melody for full orchestra, add dramatic percussion, sweeping strings, brass fanfares for a movie trailer feel.
  • Simple Folk Tune > Re-Genre to "Synthwave": Keep melody, add driving 80s drum machine beat, lush synth pads, arpeggiated basslines, and gated reverb.

Focus: Strong B2B (Film/TV/Game Composers, Advertisers); B2C (Producers, Remixers).

FOLAI - Intelligent Foley Generation & Placement

AI tool that not only generates realistic foley sounds but also automatically detects context, places sounds precisely, and modulates them within a video.

How it Works

AI analyzes video frames for actions (footsteps, doors, impacts) & environments (room size, surfaces) > Generates appropriate high-quality foley sounds > Places sounds accurately on audio timeline, synced with video action > Modulates volume, panning, and reverb based on detected distance & environment.

  • Video context analysis (object recognition, action detection)
  • Parametric sound generation or intelligent library selection
  • Automatic, precise time synchronization
  • Environment-aware audio modulation (e.g., reverb matching room size)

Examples

  • Character walks across room, opens drawer, picks up glass: FOLAI adds synced footsteps (auto-detecting wood floor sound), wood drawer sliding SFX, and a gentle glass handling sound with appropriate volume.
  • Fight scene in a rainy alley: FOLAI adds various thuds, impacts, clothing rustle, maybe specific weapon sounds, layering realistic rain sounds that vary with intensity and distance, adding echo from walls.

Focus: Broad B2C/Prosumer (YouTubers, Indie Filmmakers); Strong B2B (Editors, Animators, Game Devs).

AITTRIBUTE - Transparent AI Music Attribution

A robust engine to analyze AI-generated music and trace potential compositional influences or substantial similarities within a given training dataset.

How it Works

User inputs training dataset details (or hashes/fingerprints) & a generated music piece > AI performs comparative analysis focusing on melodic contours, harmonic progressions, rhythmic patterns, and potentially timbral similarities > Generates a report detailing potential connections, statistical likelihood of influence, and specific data points involved.

  • Addresses the "black box" problem with data lineage
  • Provides evidence for copyright compliance and reporting
  • Enables fair compensation models based on contribution
  • Focuses on compositional similarity, not just audio fingerprinting

Use Case Example

  • Scenario: A user generates a track after training a model on a dataset including licensed loops and public domain classical recordings.
  • AITTRIBUTE Report: Flags specific licensed loops whose melodic/rhythmic structure appears significantly (>X% similarity threshold) in the output, providing data for royalty calculations. May also note minor similarities to classical motifs below reporting thresholds.
  • Benefit: Allows platform/label to manage licenses and ensure compliance transparently.

Focus: B2B (AI Music Platforms, Labels, Legal/Compliance). Potential standalone service.

  • Analyzes input audio for musical context

  • Generates complementary instrumentation (drums, bass, chords, melodies)

Examples

  • Rapper: Uploads vocal acapella > Prompt: "Create a dark trap beat, 808 bass, hi-hat rolls, minor key synth melody." > Output: Complete beat ready for final mixing.
  • Podcaster/Creator: Uploads voiceover > Prompt: "Generate a chill, background lo-fi hip hop track with a simple beat and jazz piano chords." > Output: Custom intro/outro/bed music.

Focus: B2C (Musicians, Producers, Creators), B2B (Production Houses, Ad Agencies needing quick custom tracks), Trend (AI-assisted music creation).
